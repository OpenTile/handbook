# Process

## Principles

- Keep the process simple but explicit.
- Prefer small, atomic tasks.
- Review progress every week.
- Adjust priorities as needed.
- Document decisions in markdown.

## Iteration cadence

We work in weekly iterations.

Why weekly:

- faster feedback
- more opportunities to improve the process
- clearer sense of progress

## Weekly sync agenda

1. Demo what was completed during the week
2. Discuss what worked well and what did not
3. Plan work for the next week
4. Revisit project priorities
5. Create or update tasks

## Daily async check-ins

Instead of daily calls, we use daily audio updates.

Suggested format:

- what I completed
- what I am doing next
- blockers
- questions / where help is needed

## Task sizing

Tasks should be as small and atomic as possible.

This helps:

- maintain momentum
- make review easier
- reduce the risk of spending a full week on one invisible chunk of work
